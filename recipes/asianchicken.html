Asian Chicken Lettuce Wrap

DESCRIPTION

Low in carbs but still packed with delicious protein — this Asian Chicken Lettuce Wrap is the perfect no-rice starter or substitute for a main course! At American-Chinese restaurants like P.F. Chang’s, chicken lettuce wrap is the usual fare. Cups of lettuce are filled heartily with shredded chicken, diced veggies, and a savory, sticky sauce that makes your mouth water. Think of it like a tiny lumpia — just without the lumpia wrapper! The chicken lettuce wrap is the way to go for something both light and satisfying at the same time.

Quick and easy to make from home, this Asian chicken lettuce wrap will surely become a new favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1 head lettuce
  • 450 grams CDO Homestyle Chicken Flakes in Oil
  • ½ cup green onion chopped
  • 1 onion minced
  • ¼ cup carrot minced
  • 1 cup jicama diced (singkamas)
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 ½ tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash the lettuce with running water and peel the leaves apart. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok. Saute onion for 35 seconds.
  3. Add Chicken Flakes. Continue cooking until the liquid evaporates completely.
  4. Add carrot and jicama. Cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, and hoisin sauce. Stir.
  6. Put the green onions into the wok and season with salt and ground black pepper as needed.
  7. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve with fresh lettuce.
  8. Serve. Share and enjoy!
